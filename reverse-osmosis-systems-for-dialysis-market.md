<p><p>Reverse osmosis systems for dialysis are crucial for purifying water used in renal procedures. "Single Pass" systems treat water in one continuous flow, effectively removing contaminants in a single operation, making them efficient and compact for real-time use. Conversely, "Twin Pass" systems involve two stages of filtration, where water passes through the system twice, enhancing purity but requiring more space and time. Both types cater to different healthcare facility needs, balancing efficiency and water quality in dialysis treatments.</p></p>
