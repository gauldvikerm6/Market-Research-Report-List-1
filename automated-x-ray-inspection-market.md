<p><p>The Automated X-ray Inspection (AXI) market comprises two primary types: 2D AXI and 3D AXI. 2D AXI utilizes flat, traditional imaging techniques to detect defects in electronic assemblies, offering faster processing and simpler setup. In contrast, 3D AXI employs advanced imaging technologies to provide depth perception and enhance defect detection capabilities, identifying hidden anomalies that 2D systems might miss. Both types are essential for ensuring quality control in manufacturing, particularly in the electronics and automotive industries.</p></p>
