<p><p>The flexible metal hose market comprises primarily two types: strip-wound hoses and corrugated hoses. Strip-wound hoses are made by spirally winding metal strips, providing excellent flexibility and protection against mechanical stress, ideal for applications requiring high durability. Corrugated hoses, on the other hand, feature a series of ridges and grooves, allowing for greater flexibility and easier handling, making them suitable for conveying fluids and gases under varying pressures. Both types serve diverse industrial applications, enhancing performance and safety.</p></p>
