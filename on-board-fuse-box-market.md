<p><p>The on-board fuse box market is categorized into two main types: interior and under-hood. The interior fuse box is typically found inside the cabin of the vehicle, protecting electronic components like the dashboard and infotainment systems. In contrast, the under-hood fuse box is located in the engine compartment, safeguarding critical systems such as the ignition and battery connections. Both types serve to manage electrical circuits, enhancing vehicle safety and reliability by preventing overload and short circuits.</p></p>
