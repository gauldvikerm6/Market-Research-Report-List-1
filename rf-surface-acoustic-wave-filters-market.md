<p><p>The RF Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Filters market includes two main types: Transversal Filters and Resonator Filters. Transversal Filters utilize multiple interdigital transducers to achieve high selectivity and impedance matching, making them ideal for communication applications. Resonator Filters, on the other hand, rely on individual SAW resonators for filtering, offering compact designs and enhanced frequency stability suited for various electronic devices. Both types are integral for signal processing in telecommunications, consumer electronics, and automotive applications.</p></p>
