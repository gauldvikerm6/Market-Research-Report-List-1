<p><p>Ceramic packaging materials are categorized into three main types based on their properties and applications. Mechanical ceramic materials, known for their strength and toughness, are used in structural components and protective casings. Thermal ceramic materials, which excel in heat resistance and insulation, are essential for applications in electronics and aerospace. Electrical ceramic materials, characterized by their dielectric properties, are vital for insulators and capacitors in electronic devices. Each type plays a critical role in enhancing performance and durability across various industries.</p></p>
