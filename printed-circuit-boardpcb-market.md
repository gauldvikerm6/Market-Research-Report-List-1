<p><p>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) market is categorized into three main types: Single-Sided Boards, Double-Sided Boards, and Multilayer Circuit Boards. Single-Sided Boards feature components on one side, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, ideal for basic electronics. Double-Sided Boards allow components on both sides, enhancing functionality and density for more complex devices. Multilayer Circuit Boards consist of multiple layers, enabling intricate designs and high-performance applications in advanced electronics, such as computers and communication devices, catering to demanding industry needs.</p></p>
