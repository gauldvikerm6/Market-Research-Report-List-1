<p><p>The carpet yarn market consists of various materials, each offering unique properties. Nylon carpet fibers are known for durability and resilience, making them ideal for high-traffic areas. Polyester (PET) fibers offer vivid colors and stain resistance but are less durable. PTT (Triexta) fibers combine the benefits of nylon and polyester, offering softness and durability. Polypropylene (Olefin) fibers are affordable and moisture-resistant, while wool fibers provide natural warmth and luxury. Cotton fibers are eco-friendly, soft, and breathable, suitable for casual settings.</p></p>
