<p><p>The baking soda market comprises three main types: Soda Ash, Double Decomposition, and Natural Soda. Soda Ash, primarily used in glass manufacturing, is produced synthetically through the Solvay process. In Double Decomposition, baking soda is obtained from reacting a sodium salt with an acid, generating a salt and water. Natural Soda refers to sodium bicarbonate sourced from mineral deposits or natural brines. Each type has distinct production methods and applications, catering to various industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and cleaning.</p></p>
