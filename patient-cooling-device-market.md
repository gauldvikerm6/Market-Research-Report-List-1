<p><strong>The Patient Cooling Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Cooling Systems</li><li>Intravascular Cooling Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The patient cooling device market encompasses various types, primarily surface cooling systems and intravascular cooling systems. Surface cooling systems utilize external pads or blankets that circulate cooled fluids to lower the patient's body temperature, primarily for treating conditions like hyperthermia and post-cardiac arrest. Intravascular cooling systems, on the other hand, involve the infusion of cooled fluids directly into the bloodstream through catheters, offering precise temperature regulation for critically ill patients and enhancing therapeutic outcomes. Both methods aim to improve patient recovery and comfort.</p></p>
