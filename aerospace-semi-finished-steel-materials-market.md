<p><p>The aerospace semi-finished steel materials market encompasses various product forms essential for aircraft manufacturing and components. Plates are thicker than sheets, used for parts requiring extra durability, such as landing gear. Tubes are hollow sections utilized in frameworks and fuel lines, offering strength while minimizing weight, crucial for aerospace applications.</p></p>
