<p><ul><li>Prestressed Concrete Cylinder Pipe</li><li>Prestressed Reinforced Concrete Pipe</li><li>Reinforced Concrete Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete pipe market comprises several types, including Prestressed Concrete Cylinder Pipe (PCCP), which is designed for high-pressure applications with a steel cylinder providing strength and durability. Prestressed Reinforced Concrete Pipe (PRCP) incorporates prestressing techniques and reinforced concrete, enhancing its resistance to internal pressure and external loads. Reinforced Concrete Pipe (RCP) is commonly used for drainage and sewage systems, featuring embedded steel reinforcement to increase structural integrity. Each type serves specific engineering needs, balancing strength, flexibility, and cost considerations.</p></p>
