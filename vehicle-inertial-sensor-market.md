<p><strong>The Vehicle Inertial Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acceleration Sensor</li><li>Gyroscope</li><li>IMU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Inertial Sensor Market includes three main types: acceleration sensors, gyroscopes, and inertial measurement units (IMUs). Acceleration sensors measure changes in speed and direction of a vehicle, providing vital data for stability control and safety systems. Gyroscopes monitor the vehicle's rotational movement, enhancing navigation and stability during maneuvers. IMUs combine accelerometers and gyroscopes to provide comprehensive motion data, enabling advanced applications like autonomous driving and advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS), thereby improving overall vehicle performance and safety.</p></p>
