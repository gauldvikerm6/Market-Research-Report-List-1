<p><ul><li>Flat Airbag Fabric</li><li>OPW Airbag Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passenger vehicle airbag fabric market primarily consists of two types: flat airbag fabric and OPW (One Point Weave) airbag fabric. Flat airbag fabric is characterized by its simple, uniform structure, providing effective deployment and cushioning during a collision. On the other hand, OPW airbag fabric features a more complex weaving pattern, enhancing strength and durability while allowing for better inflation. Both types are crucial in ensuring passenger safety by minimizing injury during accidents, adhering to stringent automotive standards.</p></p>
