<p><p>The juice extractor machine market comprises several types, primarily including centrifugal juicers and masticating juicers. Centrifugal juicers operate at high speeds, shredding fruits and vegetables to extract juice quickly, making them convenient for everyday use. In contrast, masticating juicers work at slower speeds, crushing produce to retain more nutrients and yield drier pulp, often preferred for higher-quality juice. Other types may include triturating or twin-gear juicers, which offer unique extraction methods for specific applications and consumer preferences.</p></p>
