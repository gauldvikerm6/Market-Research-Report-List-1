<p><p>Nanoscale drug delivery devices are classified based on their responsiveness to various stimuli. Temperature response devices release drugs in response to temperature changes, enhancing targeting and efficacy. Acid-base pH response devices react to specific pH levels in the body, delivering drugs in acidic or basic environments. Redox response devices utilize oxidation-reduction reactions to trigger drug release, while magnetic field response devices employ magnetic forces for targeted delivery. Other types may include ultrasound- or light-responsive systems, broadening therapeutic applications.</p></p>
