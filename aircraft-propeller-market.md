<p><strong>The Aircraft Propeller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed-Pitch Propeller</li><li>Test Club Propeller</li><li>Ground-Adjustable Propeller</li><li>Controllable-Pitch Propeller</li><li>Constant-Speed Propeller</li><li>Feathering Propeller</li><li>Reverse-Pitch Propeller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft propeller market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Fixed-Pitch Propeller**: Offers a set blade angle, providing simplicity but limiting performance.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Test Club Propeller**: Used for testing engine performance with variable configurations.</p><p>3. **Ground-Adjustable Propeller**: Allows adjustment of the blade pitch on the ground for optimized performance.</p><p>4. **Controllable-Pitch Propeller**: Change pitch during flight for better efficiency.</p><p>5. **Constant-Speed Propeller**: Maintains optimal speed across various conditions automatically.</p><p>6. **Feathering Propeller**: Blades align for minimal drag in case of an engine failure.</p><p>7. **Reverse-Pitch Propeller**: Enables reverse thrust for better ground maneuverability.</p></p>
