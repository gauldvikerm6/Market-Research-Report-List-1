<p><p>The luggage bag market is primarily divided into two types: hard luggage bags and soft luggage bags. Hard luggage bags are made from durable materials like polycarbonate or aluminum, offering superior protection for fragile items and resistance to impact. They typically come with a rigid structure, ensuring security but less flexibility in packing. In contrast, soft luggage bags are constructed from fabrics like nylon or canvas, allowing for greater maneuverability and expandability. They are generally lighter, making them easier to carry while providing ample storage space.</p></p>
