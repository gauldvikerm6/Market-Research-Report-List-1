<p><p>The targeted drug delivery system market encompasses three levels of targeting to enhance therapeutic efficacy and minimize side effects. First-order targeting focuses on organ compartmentalization, directing drugs to specific organs. Second-order targeting involves cellular targeting, ensuring drugs reach particular cell types for maximal impact. Third-order targeting targets intracellular sites, delivering drugs directly into cells for precise action at the molecular level. This multi-tiered approach enhances treatment outcomes across various diseases by improving drug localization and effectiveness.</p></p>
