<p><strong>The Automotive LiDAR Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Lidar</li><li>Solid State Lidar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive LiDAR scanner market comprises two main types: mechanical LiDAR and solid-state LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR uses rotating components to capture 3D environments, offering high-resolution data and wide field-of-view, making it suitable for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and autonomous vehicles. In contrast, solid-state LiDAR employs fixed components, reducing complexity and size, while enhancing reliability and durability. Solid-state systems are often more cost-effective and easier to integrate, catering to diverse automotive applications in the evolving landscape of vehicle automation.</p></p>
