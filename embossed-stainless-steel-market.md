<p><p>The embossed stainless steel market is categorized into three main series: 200, 300, and 400. The 200 Series, primarily composed of manganese and nitrogen, is cost-effective and offers good corrosion resistance. The 300 Series, known for its high nickel content, provides excellent formability and weldability, making it suitable for various applications. The 400 Series, which contains higher levels of chromium, is magnetic and offers moderate corrosion resistance, often used in environments requiring durability, such as automotive and architectural applications.</p></p>
