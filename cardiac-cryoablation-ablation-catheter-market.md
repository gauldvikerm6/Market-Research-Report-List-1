<p><p>Cardiac cryoablation ablation catheters are specialized devices used in treating arrhythmias by applying extreme cold to targeted heart tissue. The market is categorized into two main types: single point and multielectrode catheters. Single point catheters deliver cryoenergy at a specific site, allowing precise ablation but requiring multiple applications for larger areas. In contrast, multielectrode catheters feature multiple cryo-ablative points, enabling broader tissue ablation in a single application, enhancing efficiency and reducing procedural time during treatment.</p></p>
