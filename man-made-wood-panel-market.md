Particle board is composed of wood chips and adhesive, making it cost-effective. Plywood features layers of veneer for strength. OSB uses strands for structural applications. MDF and HDF offer smooth surfaces ideal for furniture and cabinetry. Hardboard, made from compressed fibers, is often used in protective and decorative applications. Together, these composites serve diverse construction and furniture needs.</p></p>
