<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial metallic paints market is divided into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based metallic paints typically offer superior durability and finish, making them suitable for high-performance applications; however, they may contain volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, water-based metallic paints are more environmentally friendly, with lower VOC levels, making them safer for indoor use. While they may have slightly less durability than solvent-based options, they are increasingly favored for their ease of cleanup and lower environmental impact.</p></p>
