<p><p>The thermoset adhesives market comprises various types, each suited for specific applications. Phenolic Formaldehyde (PF) resins offer strong bonding for high-temperature applications, while Phenolic Neoprene combines flexibility with heat resistance. Resorcinol Formaldehydes (RF) provide excellent waterproofing, making them ideal for wood and composite bonding. Polyesters are versatile and cost-effective, used in diverse industries. Polyimides excel in extreme environments due to their thermal stability, and Epoxy Resins are known for their exceptional strength and durability, widely utilized in construction and aerospace sectors.</p></p>
