<p><strong>The Production Granulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Granulation</li><li>Wet Granulation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The production granulators market is primarily segmented into dry and wet granulation techniques, each serving distinct applications. Dry granulation involves compressing powders into granules without the use of liquid, ideal for temperature-sensitive materials. This method enhances flowability and compressibility. Conversely, wet granulation incorporates a binding liquid to agglomerate powders, promoting uniformity and stability in the final product. Wet granulation is often favored in pharmaceuticals for its ability to achieve desired tablet characteristics and improve drug dissolution rates.</p></p>
