<p><p>The cannabis extraction market encompasses various methods to obtain cannabinoids and terpenes. Sub/Supercritical CO2 extraction uses pressurized carbon dioxide to achieve high purity. Ethanol extraction utilizes ethanol as a solvent, ensuring effectiveness and safety. Hydrocarbon extraction employs hydrocarbons like butane for high-quality concentrates but requires careful handling. Solvent-less extraction, including methods like rosin pressing, avoids chemicals, appealing to purists. Other methods include ice water extraction or olive oil extraction, catering to different preferences and product types in the market.</p></p>
