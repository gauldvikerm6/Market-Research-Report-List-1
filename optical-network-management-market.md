<p><ul><li>Synchronous Optical Network (SONET)</li><li>Width Division Multiplexing (WDM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Network Management market encompasses various technologies for efficient data transmission over optical fibers. Synchronous Optical Network (SONET) offers standardized multiplexing for digital data transmission, ensuring reliable communication and synchronization across networks. On the other hand, Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM) enhances data capacity by allowing multiple light wavelengths to transmit simultaneously over a single fiber, optimizing bandwidth usage. Both technologies play crucial roles in improving network efficiency, scalability, and performance in telecommunications infrastructure.</p></p>
