<p><p>The cancer tumor profiling market encompasses various techniques crucial for understanding tumor genetics and developing personalized treatment plans. Next-generation sequencing (NGS) allows for comprehensive genomic analysis, identifying mutations and biomarkers. Polymerase chain reaction (PCR) amplifies specific DNA sequences for targeted analysis. Immunohistochemistry (IHC) detects proteins in tissue samples, offering insights into tumor characteristics. In situ hybridization (ISH) visualizes gene expression within cells. Microarray technology assesses multiple gene expressions simultaneously, while "Others" includes emerging methodologies, enhancing diagnostic precision and treatment options.</p></p>
