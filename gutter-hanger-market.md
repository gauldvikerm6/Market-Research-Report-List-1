<p><p>The gutter hanger market comprises three main types: wrap-around, fixed, and T-bar hangers. Wrap-around hangers provide a versatile solution by encircling the gutter, offering stability and adaptability to various shapes. Fixed hangers, designed for straightforward installation, are anchored to the fascia, ensuring secure placement without additional adjustments. T-bar hangers feature a distinctive bar design that allows for enhanced support and alignment, making them ideal for heavier gutters. Each type serves unique installation needs while ensuring effective water drainage.</p></p>
