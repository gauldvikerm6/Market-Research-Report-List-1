<p><p>The Cloud CFD (Computational Fluid Dynamics) market is divided into two main types: steady simulation and unsteady simulation. Steady simulation focuses on steady-state conditions where fluid properties remain constant over time, making it ideal for applications needing quick results. Unsteady simulation, on the other hand, addresses time-dependent fluid behavior, capturing transient effects for dynamic scenarios. Both types leverage cloud computing's scalability and computational power, allowing for efficient analysis and rapid iterations in various engineering and scientific applications.</p></p>
