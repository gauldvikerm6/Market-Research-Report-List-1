<p><p>The vibration sensors market comprises various types, including mechanical, optical, electrical, and others. Mechanical vibration sensors utilize mass-spring systems to detect vibrations through displacement. Optical vibration sensors employ laser technology, measuring changes in light waves to assess vibration levels. Electrical vibration sensors rely on piezoelectric or capacitive technologies to convert vibration into electrical signals. The "others" category includes advanced sensors like fiber-optic and MEMS-based devices, which offer specialized applications across industries such as manufacturing, aerospace, and automotive.</p></p>
