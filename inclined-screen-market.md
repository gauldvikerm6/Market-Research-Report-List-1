<p><p>Inclined screens are vital in particle separation processes, categorized into single, double, triple, and four-deck designs. A single-deck inclined screen handles one layer of material, providing basic sorting. Double-deck screens offer enhanced efficiency by allowing two distinct separation sizes simultaneously. Triple-deck screens further optimize sorting by processing three layers, ideal for complex materials. Four-deck inclined screens maximize efficiency in larger operations, enabling four separate outputs, thus significantly improving screening capacity and adaptability in various industries.</p></p>
