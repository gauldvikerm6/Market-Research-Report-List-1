<p><p>The digital manometer market features several types based on measurement methods. The absolute pressure type measures pressure relative to a perfect vacuum, providing accurate readings in various applications. The gauge pressure type measures pressure relative to atmospheric pressure, commonly used in industrial processes. Differential pressure type measures the difference between two pressure points, ideal for flow measurement and filtration systems. Other types may include multi-functional manometers or those with specialized functions, catering to diverse industry needs and applications.</p></p>
